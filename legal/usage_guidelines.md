# Usage Guidelines

## Allowed Uses

- TableUp product onboarding and empty states.
- Social posts, comics, stickers, short videos, and internal pitch decks.
- Brand education around food waste reduction and cooking confidence.

## Review Required

- Merchandise
- Paid advertising
- External licensing
- Character redesigns
- Public claims about food safety or health

## Not Allowed

- Political endorsement
- Medical or nutrition claims without review
- Horror, gore, or spoiled-food shock imagery
- Replacing the characters with inconsistent styles in official materials
