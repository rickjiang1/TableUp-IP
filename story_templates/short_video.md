# Short Video Template

## Duration

15 to 45 seconds.

## Structure

1. Hook: “冰箱里这几个快过期了，今晚怎么救？”
2. Character entrance: one hero character reacts with a catchphrase.
3. Ingredient scan: show 3 to 5 ingredients.
4. Rescue plan: one simple dish idea.
5. Payoff: warm joke, family dinner moment, or confidence line.

## Visual Notes

Use fast readable cuts, strong character expressions, and a kitchen background. Keep captions short and friendly.
