# Recipe Rescue Episode Template

## Episode Title

“冰箱救援队：____大作战”

## Problem

What ingredient is at risk? What dinner decision is blocking the family?

## Cast

Choose 2 to 4 characters. Fridge Tianshi appears when diagnosis is needed.

## Inventory

List available ingredients and urgency level.

## Rescue Recipe

Give one practical dish idea with simple steps.

## Character Beats

- One joke from personality conflict.
- One useful kitchen tip.
- One warm confidence moment.

## Ending Line

Close with a TableUp-style anti-waste sentence.
