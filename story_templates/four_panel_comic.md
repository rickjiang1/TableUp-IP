# Four-Panel Comic Template

## Panel 1: Fridge Problem

Show the ingredient crisis: something is close to expiring, dinner is undecided, or the fridge is chaotic.

## Panel 2: Character Conflict

Two or more characters disagree in their signature voices. Keep the conflict funny and low-stakes.

## Panel 3: Rescue Idea

Fridge Tianshi or another specialist proposes a practical dish using available ingredients.

## Panel 4: Warm Payoff

The team lands on a meal, a punchline, or a cooking-confidence moment.

## Required Footer

Include the moral: use what you have, waste less, cook with confidence.
