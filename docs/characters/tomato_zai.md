# Tomato Zai

## Basic Info

* Chinese name: 番茄仔
* English name: Tomato Zai
* Universe: Fridge Jianghu / TableUp Food Universe
* First appearance: Season 1, Episode 1
* Starting team: Egg Xiao Huang, Scallion Bro, Garlic Master, Tomato Zai
* Role: Hot-blooded tomato companion, emotional driver, and key ingredient for the first cooking battle.

## Personality

Tomato Zai is energetic, impulsive, proud, loyal, and a little dramatic.
He wants to prove that tomato is not just a side ingredient.
He often acts brave first and thinks later, but deep down he is warm-hearted and cares about his friends.

## Visual Design

Tomato Zai has a round bright-red tomato body, green tomato leaves shaped like messy hair, thick expressive eyebrows, large eyes, and a confident smile.
He wears a Chinese-style young hero outfit with a red waist sash, wrist wraps, and simple martial arts shoes.
His design should feel cute, heroic, funny, and suitable for a long-running cooking adventure comic.

## Catchphrases

* “Who says tomato can only be a side character?”
* “Tomato Zai attacks! Sweet, sour, and unstoppable!”

## Story Function

In Season 1 Episode 1, Carrot Hero challenges the village.
The starting available ingredients are egg, scallion, garlic, and tomato.
Under the guidance of Fridge Master, Tomato Zai works together with Egg Xiao Huang and the others to make tomato scrambled eggs and win the challenge.

## Character Arc

Tomato Zai begins as a hot-headed character who wants to prove himself.
Through cooking battles, he learns that true strength comes from teamwork, timing, and understanding how ingredients support each other.
