# TableUp Kitchen Universe / 冰箱救援队宇宙

## Premise

Inside every Chinese family kitchen lives a tiny order of ingredient guardians. When leftovers are forgotten, vegetables begin to wilt, or the family asks “今晚吃什么,” the 冰箱救援队 wakes up.

Their mission is simple: rescue ingredients before they are wasted, turn ordinary fridge inventory into dinner ideas, and help people feel calm and confident about cooking.

## Themes

- 减少浪费: every ingredient deserves a second chance.
- 利用库存: the best recipe often starts with what is already at home.
- 今晚吃什么: decision fatigue becomes a story problem the team can solve.
- 做饭信心: cooking is not perfection; it is care, timing, and a little courage.

## Tone

Warm, humorous, family-friendly, and lightly theatrical. The universe should feel rooted in Chinese household kitchens: fridge magnets, chopping boards, steam, rice bowls, market bags, leftovers, pantry jars, and weeknight urgency.

## Visual Direction

- 3D picture-book characters with soft materials and readable silhouettes.
- Expressive facial acting suitable for comics and short-form animation.
- Warm kitchen lighting, clean backgrounds, tactile food textures.
- Chinese cultural details used with restraint: robes, seals, tea cups, fans, market scooters, aprons, handwritten labels.

## Story Engine

Each episode begins with a fridge problem: expiring food, mismatched ingredients, no dinner plan, a picky eater, or a family member losing confidence. Fridge Tianshi diagnoses the situation, the team argues with personality, and the ingredients combine into a practical dish idea.

## Canon Rule

Food is never mocked for being imperfect. Humor comes from personality clashes, kitchen timing, and exaggerated ingredient emotions, not from shame around cooking skill or household habits.
