# Character Bible

This document is the index for the 冰箱救援队 core cast. Each character also has a standalone Bible in `characters/<character>/README.md` and production prompts in `characters/<character>/prompts.md`.

## Ensemble Roles

- Fridge Tianshi leads diagnosis and keeps the fridge universe in balance.
- Garlic Master brings calm, depth, and flavor wisdom.
- Scallion Bro gathers market information and fast kitchen intelligence.
- Ginger Sis cuts through hesitation and warms the team with practical courage.
- Meat Bro protects protein ingredients and gives the team physical confidence.
- Fish Uncle offers patient, precise timing and seafood knowledge.
- Egg Xiaohuang makes freshness anxiety visible and lovable.
- Chili Sis raises energy, appetite, and dramatic stakes.

## Relationship Dynamics

- Fridge Tianshi and Garlic Master are the elder strategists.
- Scallion Bro and Chili Sis create fast, loud, comic momentum.
- Meat Bro is protective toward Egg Xiaohuang.
- Fish Uncle slows down chaotic scenes and reframes problems calmly.
- Ginger Sis is the practical middle voice who turns talk into action.

## Canon Prompt Style

All character prompts should include: anthropomorphic ingredient, warm Chinese family kitchen universe, 3D picture-book style, expressive face, clean background, full body character design, animation-ready silhouette, high detail, soft lighting.

## Extended Character: 洋葱哥 / Onion Bro

Onion Bro is a calm, Buddhist-style onion wisdom character. He works best as a cooling presence in chaotic kitchen scenes, a one-line comic closer, and a relaxed supporting role who helps the team stop overthinking.

Canon note: Onion Bro is preserved as an extended character because a reference image was supplied, while the requested core cast uses 姜姐 / Ginger Sis as one of the eight primary folders.

## Starting Ingredient Companion: 番茄仔 / Tomato Zai

Tomato Zai is a hot-blooded tomato companion in the Fridge Jianghu / TableUp Food Universe. He first appears in Season 1, Episode 1 as part of the starting team with Egg Xiao Huang, Scallion Bro, and Garlic Master. His story role is to bring emotional momentum to the first cooking battle and help turn egg, scallion, garlic, and tomato into tomato scrambled eggs under Fridge Master’s guidance.

## Companion Character: 牛大壮 / Niu Dazhuang

Niu Dazhuang is a reliable hard-bodied companion in the Fridge Jianghu / TableUp Food Universe. He is steady, direct, loyal, and protective, bringing physical support and dependable warmth to cooking battles. His role is to reassure the team, defend weaker companions, and turn kitchen chaos into practical action.
