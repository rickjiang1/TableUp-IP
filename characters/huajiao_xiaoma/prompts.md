Anthropomorphic Sichuan pepper aroma spirit, small cracked reddish-brown pepper husk head with dotted texture, lively eyes, mischievous smile, pepper berries as ornaments, holding a pepper-berry staff, agile martial companion outfit in warm brown and red tones, tiny electric aroma sparks around fingertips, playful confident pose, Fridge Jianghu cooking adventure universe, 3D picture-book character concept sheet, warm parchment background, family-friendly, animation-ready design.

## Expression Sheet Prompt

Create an expression sheet for Huajiao Xiaoma: proud, playful, electric spark, serious timing, surprised, dizzy from numbing energy, and triumphant. Keep the same pepper husk silhouette, berry staff, costume, colors, and 3D picture-book style.

## Negative Prompt

Avoid horror, insects, realistic skin pores, brand logos, app UI screenshots, extra limbs, or making the pepper texture look rotten.
