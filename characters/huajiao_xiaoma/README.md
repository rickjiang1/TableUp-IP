## Personality

Huajiao Xiaoma is nimble, playful, quick-witted, curious, and a little mischievous.
She reacts fast, jokes easily, and loves surprising others with a delayed burst of numbing flavor.
She can seem cheeky on the surface, but she is reliable when timing matters and will always jump in when a friend needs help.

Her core emotional trait is lively precision: she looks chaotic, but her best moments come from knowing exactly when to release the spark.
She must learn that good flavor is not only about being noticed, but about arriving at the right second.

## Visual Design

Huajiao Xiaoma has a cracked Sichuan pepper husk head with dotted pepper texture, warm reddish-brown tones, bright lively eyes, mischievous smile, and small pepper berries on her staff and costume.
Her body language is light and springy, with one foot often lifted as if she is about to bounce away.
Her design should feel electric, aromatic, cute, clever, and suitable for animated action beats.

## Story Function

Huajiao Xiaoma brings speed, sensory surprise, and comic timing to Fridge Jianghu stories.
She is useful when a dish needs aroma, brightness, and a memorable final lift.
In team scenes, she can interrupt heavy moments with quick humor, then prove that her “small” flavor can change the whole battle.

## Character Arc

Huajiao Xiaoma begins as a character who wants everyone to notice her spark immediately.
Through cooking battles, she learns patience, restraint, and the power of delayed impact.
Her growth is about turning restless energy into precise timing.
