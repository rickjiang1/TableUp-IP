# Pixian Zhanggui

## Basic Info

* Chinese name: 郫县掌柜
* English name: Pixian Zhanggui
* Universe: Fridge Jianghu / TableUp Food Universe
* Character type: Doubanjiang master shopkeeper
* Role: Red-oil base-flavor mentor, fermentation wisdom keeper, and authority on deep savory foundations.
* Asset: `characters/pixian_zhanggui/reference/pixian_zhanggui_concept_sheet.png`

## Personality

Pixian Zhanggui is steady, seasoned, principled, observant, and quietly formidable.
He speaks slowly, rarely wastes a word, and cares deeply about rules, timing, and proper foundations.
He can look stern, but his strictness comes from craft pride and a desire to help younger ingredients understand depth.

His core emotional trait is patient authority: he believes flavor cannot be rushed.
He teaches others that true aroma is built through time, heat control, and respect for ingredients.

## Visual Design

Pixian Zhanggui is a stout red-oil doubanjiang shopkeeper with a round sauce-colored face, wise half-lidded eyes, dark mustache and beard, ornate red-brown robe, sauce jar motifs, and a wooden spoon.
His costume should feel like an old Sichuan sauce shop master: heavy, grounded, fragrant, and full of tradition.
The design should be warm, humorous, authoritative, and instantly readable as a base-flavor master.

## Catchphrases

* “Aroma is not added. It is simmered out.”
* “You can have numbing and spicy, but you still need foundation.”

## Common Actions

* Lifts a wooden spoon to judge the depth of a sauce.
* Watches ingredients with a calm, knowing expression.
* Opens a sauce jar and lets red oil aroma rise.
* Gives one precise rule before a cooking battle.
* Takes control of the scene with a single measured move.

## Story Function

Pixian Zhanggui brings depth, craft tradition, and culinary authority to Fridge Jianghu.
He is ideal for stories about building flavor foundations, respecting process, and turning loud spicy energy into balanced taste.
He can mentor younger characters, judge cooking battles, or appear when a dish lacks “bottom flavor.”

## Character Arc

Pixian Zhanggui begins as a strict keeper of tradition who believes correct rules come first.
Over time, he learns to let younger ingredients experiment while still protecting the soul of the craft.
His growth is about balancing old flavor discipline with new creative energy.
