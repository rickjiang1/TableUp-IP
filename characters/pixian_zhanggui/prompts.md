# Pixian Zhanggui Prompts

## Base Character Prompt

Anthropomorphic Pixian doubanjiang shopkeeper master, stout sauce-colored body and face, wise half-lidded eyes, dark mustache and small beard, ornate red-brown robe with red oil patterns, shopkeeper headband, holding a wooden spoon with fermented chili bean paste, sauce jar beside him, steady authoritative expression, Sichuan base flavor mentor, Fridge Jianghu cooking adventure universe, 3D picture-book character concept sheet, warm parchment background, family-friendly, animation-ready design.

## Expression Sheet Prompt

Create an expression sheet for Pixian Zhanggui: steady, judging flavor, explaining rules, satisfied, taking action, stern, and commanding the kitchen. Keep the same sauce-shopkeeper silhouette, wooden spoon, jar, red-oil robe, and 3D picture-book style.

## Negative Prompt

Avoid horror, dirty spoiled sauce, brand logos, app UI screenshots, extra limbs, or making the character look villainous.
