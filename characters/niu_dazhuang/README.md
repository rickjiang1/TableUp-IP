# Niu Dazhuang

## Basic Info

* Chinese name: 牛大壮
* English name: Niu Dazhuang
* Universe: Fridge Jianghu / TableUp Food Universe
* Character type: Reliable hard-bodied companion
* Role: Defensive teammate, physical support, and hearty energy provider for cooking battles.
* Asset: `characters/niu_dazhuang/reference/niu_dazhuang_concept_sheet.png`

## Personality

Niu Dazhuang is steady, reliable, direct, warm-hearted, and deeply loyal.
He does not show off with clever words, but he always appears when the team needs help.
He is brave without being reckless, serious when protecting friends, and gentle when someone is hungry or tired.

His core emotional trait is dependable warmth: he wants everyone to feel safe, fed, and supported.
He believes promises matter, teamwork matters, and no teammate should be left behind.

## Visual Design

Niu Dazhuang is a young heroic ox character with a strong compact body, large expressive eyes, thick eyebrows, curved horns, brown patterned skin, and a confident smile.
He wears a Chinese-style martial companion outfit with a cream vest, red headband, red waist sash, wrist wraps, simple pants, and sturdy shoes.
His silhouette should feel solid, protective, cute, and instantly readable in comic panels.

## Story Function

Niu Dazhuang brings security and physical momentum to the Fridge Jianghu cast.
He is the teammate who can hold the line during chaotic cooking battles, protect weaker companions, and turn nervous energy into action.
He works especially well in stories about teamwork, courage, replenishing energy, and reliable support.

## Character Arc

Niu Dazhuang begins as someone who believes strength means taking responsibility for everything himself.
Over time, he learns that true reliability is not carrying every burden alone, but helping the team trust each other.
His growth is about balancing toughness with tenderness, and learning when to defend, when to step back, and when to let others shine.
