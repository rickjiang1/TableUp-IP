# Social Media Posts

## Brand Voice

Warm, witty, practical, and lightly dramatic. The content should make users feel that dinner can start from what they already have.

## Post Ideas

- “今晚冰箱救援”：show 3 ingredients and ask the audience what dish they would make.
- “角色说菜”：one character gives a short cooking tip in their voice.
- “别浪费挑战”：rescue one overlooked ingredient each day.
- “冰箱天师判案”：turn food storage mistakes into humorous micro-stories.
